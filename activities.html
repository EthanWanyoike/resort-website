Radiant Island Resort

Your #1 Holiday Resort!

Few people will want to spend an entire holiday sunbathing and swimming, so we have program of excursions and activities for our guests.

Activities available for our guests include:

  • Day trips - Day trips are one of the very few ways you’ll be able to see something of the 'real' Maldives occupied by ordinary Maldivians. This is a good way to see otherwise inaccessible islands.
  • Fishing - Our customers get to do sunset, sunrise or night-fishing trips. We can also arrange big-game fishing trips. Large boats, fully equipped with radar technology, are used to catch dorado, tuna, marlin, barracuda and jackfish among others.
  • Snorkelling & Diving - our resort caters for divers and snorkellers, and most organise twice-daily diving excursions and sometimes snorkelling trips too. Dive schools are generally of an exceptionally high safety standard, as regulated by Maldivian laws.
  • Spa Treatments - As a destination that has become synonymous with relaxation, our Maldivian resort unsurprisingly offers a huge array of treatments in purpose-built spas. These include many different types of massage, beauty treatments, Ayurvedic (Indian herbal) medicine, acupuncture and even traditional Maldivian treatments.
  • Surfing - Our resort is very popular with surfers. With surfer arrivals going up, we are located near 3 good breaks. For those wishing to avoid the crowds and explore a pristine region of the country we offer an option to join a ‘surfari’.
